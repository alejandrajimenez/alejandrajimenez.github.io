About

Hey there!
So, here's a little bit about me:
I have a background in baking, I actually graduated from
Le Cordon Bleu College Of Culinary Arts in Miami in 2012.

I worked in NYC in many bakeries and cake shops, but due to
developing sensitivities and dietary restrictions, I decided to work
on my own menu which soon turned into a pretty cool bakery project!
Hopefully, the bakery should open sometime next year.

In the meantime, I decided to go back to school and enrolled in
University of Miami to pursue my studies in Fine Arts and Psychology.
My expected graduation date is Summer 2017.
